Eggs

Description:

Scrambles eggs seasoned with pepper, pink salt and chrushed red pepper flakes topped with diced avacado and peprika.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs scrambled
  • pink salt
  • pepper
  • crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 avacado
  • peprika

Steps:

  1. Put scrambled eggs in a medium hot pan stir continuously
  2. Once starting to harden up while still a little most add the pink salt, pepper and red crushed pepper flakes
  3. Dice avacado
  4. In bowl toss avacado and peprika together
  5. places eggs froim plan onto a plate top with avacado.. enjoy
