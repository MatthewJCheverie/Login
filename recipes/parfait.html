Parfait

Description:

Plain greek yogurt parfait topped with seeds, starwberries, blueberries, granola amd dark choclate chips

Ingredients:

  • plain greek yogurt
  • hemp seeds
  • starwberries
  • blueberries
  • granola
  • dark choclate chips

Steps:

  1. Put greek yogurt in a bowl
  2. Wash starwberries and blueberries, dice strawberrys
  3. Add granola, seeds and choclate chips to the bowl of yogurt
  4. Add the blueberries and strawberries... enjoy
