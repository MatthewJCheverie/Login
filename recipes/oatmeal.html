Oatmeal

Description:

Oatmeal topped with banannas, seeds, walnuts, peanutbutter and cinnamon

Ingredients:

  • instant oatmeal
  • 1 bananna
  • chia seeds
  • walnuts
  • peanut butter
  • cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Boil water place onto oatmeal mixed with cinnamon let set for three minutes
  2. Add a scoop of peanut butter, handful of walnuts, sprinle the chia seeds over the top
  3. Cut bananna
  4. Add bananna... enjoy
